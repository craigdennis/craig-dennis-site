The 5-Section Scan Framework

This is the core engine.

1

Bottom-Funnel Demand Snapshot

What we check:

  • Competitor + “vs” terms
  • Alternative comparisons
  • “Best [category]” terms
  • “[Competitor] pricing”
  • “[Competitor] alternatives”

What we show: Which terms exist, who ranks, whether aggregators dominate, whether you appear. Directional insight is enough.

Example output: You currently rank for 12% of evaluation-stage demand in your category. Numbers create discomfort.

2

Comparison Coverage Audit

We check: Do you have competitor pages? Are they structured? Do you control framing — or are competitors defining you?

We score: Presence, structure, narrative control, objection handling. This exposes structural absence.

3

Aggregator Exposure Analysis

We look for: G2, Capterra, industry directories, affiliate review sites.

We show: Where aggregators rank above you. Where decision framing is outsourced.

Positioned as: Who defines the deal — you or third parties? Very powerful psychologically.

4

Conversion Architecture Check

We review: CTA clarity, proof placement, pricing transparency, objection handling, stage-appropriate messaging. Light-touch — we don’t redesign. We highlight friction.

5

Sales Narrative Alignment Check

We ask (or infer): What objections does sales hear? Are those addressed on evaluation pages? Is messaging aligned? We expose disconnects. This sets up the Sales Enablement Integration™ from the Sprint.
